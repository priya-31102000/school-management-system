School motto:


The motto of AYIRP SCHOOL is “Grow with Excellence “.The minds of the young are moulded and motivated by our devoted, hard working staff members who value professional development, current instrumental strategies and methods with which they prepare themselves to do their best with our students. Our energetic duty conscious non-teaching staff have always been of great help and their sincere service has contributed much to the smooth running of our school.


Aim of the school:


To make students academically competent by evolving creative teaching methods that will kindle their curiosity and inspire them to become life –long learner.


Providing relevant information at all levels with the use of audio-visual and other sensorial aids.


To create in the students, social awareness and a sense of belongingness by encouraging them to assume leadership and contribute towards uplifting local and world communities.
