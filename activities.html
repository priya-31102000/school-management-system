SPORTS

Our students at all levels are strongly encouraged to participate in various sports activities. Sports are fun and offer student the opportunity to develop skills, make lasting friendship, and learn the values of discipline and sportsmanship. Sports are also important in order to promote physical fitness and competitiveness among students. Physical education which encourages and enables the student to develop their educational status as well as personality is being treated with great concern as a result of which students are continuously bagging prizes in zonal, District and Divisional level sports meet. Our girls senior throw ball team participated in state level match at Tiruchengode.

English Literary Club

English Literary Club provides an opportunity for the students to exhibit their talent. Competitions like Quiz, Debates and Essay are held every month by the club in the school. The student are also trained for such competitions at the inter school level. For important events, a chief guest is invited and students are motivated and innovative ideas from the speeches made.



Science Club

The Science club activities are held every month. The science teacher gives option to each of the student to choose a topic of their interest on a rotational basis. The selected topic would then be discussed with each student providing her input to the discussion. Working models and group activities would be done by the student under the guidance of the teacher. Science club activities help the student to bring out their hidden knowledge helping them gain experience for their future.



Mathematics Club

The mathematics club is the place where students learn and explore mathematical concepts and verify mathematical facts through a variety of activity. Students are encouraged to collect information about great mathematicians. Every student involves in the activities of the club and gain experience for their future.



Eco Club

The school Eco Club plays an important role in creating environmental awareness among the future generation. The coordinators and club members have under taken various environmental friendly activities including Solid waste Disposal Help in pollution control Work on prevention of the storage water. Planting trees Celebrate festivals like would environment day ,world population day, world Ozone day



Junior red cross

The main objectives of Red Cross society are-Health, Service and Friendship In Ou School, about 42 student are registered as members of the JRC. Teacher Smt.Maheswari, Smt. Punithavalli have been trained in red cross.
